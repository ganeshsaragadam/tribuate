About The Tech Machine

☛ Pichai Sundararajan (born June 10, 1972), better known as Sundar Pichai, is an Indian-born American business executive.He is the chief executive officer (CEO) of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google.

☛ Pichai was born in Madurai, India, in 1972. He studied engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur and Stanford University. After graduating from Stanford, he worked as a management consultant at McKinsey & Company.

☛ In 2004, Pichai joined Google as a product manager. He quickly rose through the ranks, and he was named CEO in 2015.

☛ Under Pichai's leadership, Google has continued to innovate and grow. The company has launched new products and services, such as Google Cloud Platform and Google AI. It has also expanded its reach into new markets, such as India and Africa.

☛ Pichai is a strong advocate for diversity and inclusion. He has spoken out about the importance of creating a more inclusive workplace, and he has taken steps to make Google more diverse.

☛ Pichai is a role model for young people around the world. He is a reminder that anyone can achieve their dreams, regardless of their background.
